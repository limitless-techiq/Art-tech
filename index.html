About

Art-Tech is a team of architecture, decoration, and design enthusiasts who got together to establish a high-tech print house based in Erbil, KRI. We offer our services throughout Iraq and specialize in bringing to life all forms of artwork, be it traditional or avant-garde. We utilize the latest Printing technologies, and advanced wide format printing technologies to offer the Iraqi businesses and households all that they need when it comes to decoration variety. Our services also include ornaments for hotels, resorts, offices, and villas as well as wallpaper printing, murals and more.

MISSION

To guarantee commitment and quality of our work with our clients around Iraq and provide them with services that they deserve and can trust.
